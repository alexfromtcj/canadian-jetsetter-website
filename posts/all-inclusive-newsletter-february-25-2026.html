Hola, Travellers! 🌍

While this newsletter is focused on bringing you the best all-inclusive escapes, your safety always comes first. As you’ve likely heard, there was civil unrest in parts of Mexico beginning on February 22nd.

While conditions have improved and airports have reopened to flights, it’s very important to check the Government of Canada’s Travel Advice and Advisories for Mexico to stay updated on current recommendations and safety information.
