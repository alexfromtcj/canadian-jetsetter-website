4 Limited-Time Credit Card Offers About to Expire in Canada!

Some of the best credit card offers in Canada aren’t meant to last forever — and right now, several elevated welcome bonuses are quietly set to disappear.

Over the next week and a half, four popular credit cards will see their current promotional offers expire. That means smaller welcome bonuses, fewer perks, or missed opportunities to earn hundreds (sometimes over a thousand) dollars in travel value if you wait too long.

In this post, I’ll walk you through four credit cards with limited-time offers that expire by January 1, 2026, what makes each one compelling, and who they’re actually worth it for — so you can decide whether it makes sense to apply before the clock runs out.

Critical Warnings Every Canadian Should Know

Before jumping into any of these cards, a few rules matter:

  • Never carry a balance just to earn points
  • Set calendar reminders before the second-year fee posts
  • Only apply for cards where you can naturally hit the minimum spend
  • Use your points — don’t hoard them while programs quietly devalue

These cards are tools, not traps — but only if used correctly.

Final Thoughts

Limited-time credit card offers come and go, but the smartest travel strategies are built around timing. When elevated bonuses are ending, that’s often the moment real value is created — not through spending more, but by choosing the right card at the right time.

Not every card on this list will make sense for every traveller, and that’s perfectly fine. The goal isn’t to collect cards — it’s to align the right offer with your real-world spending, travel goals, and financial comfort.

If one of these cards was already on your radar, the upcoming January 1, 2026 deadlines make this a good moment to act. If not, there will always be another opportunity ahead — and being aware of these expiring offers puts you one step ahead the next time they appear.
