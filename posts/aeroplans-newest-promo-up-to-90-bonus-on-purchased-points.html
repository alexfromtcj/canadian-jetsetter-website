Aeroplan has launched another strong promotion, great if you need to top up your points or are wanting to redeem for a specific trip.

From now until March 19, 2026 at 11:59pm PT, Aeroplan members can receive up to a 90% bonus when purchasing points. Some members may receive lower bonus offers.

Current Promotion Details

Outside of promotional periods, Aeroplan sells points at a base rate of 3.75 cents per point (CAD). During this promotion, you could acquire points for as low as 1.97 cents per point (CAD) with the 90% bonus.

Here's how the bonus tiers break down:

  • Buy 4,000–15,000 points: Get a 40% bonus
  • Buy 20,000–60,000 points: Get a 60% bonus
  • Buy 70,000+ points: Get a 90% bonus

Reminder that some members may receive lower bonus offers, so you're bonus tier break down could potentially be different.

Purchase Limits

Aeroplan allows members to purchase up to 1,000,000 points per calendar year – one of the most generous limits among loyalty programs.

For this promotion specifically, the per-transaction limit has been temporarily increased from the usual 250,000 base points to 500,000 base points per transaction.

Who Should Buy Aeroplan Points?

If you're strategic about travel rewards, buying points during a strong promotion like this can be a game-changer for your travel plans. Here are the key scenarios where purchasing points makes sense:

Reach Your Dream Destination Faster

Are you just a few thousand points short of that trip to Europe or Asia you've been planning? Instead of waiting months to accumulate more points through everyday spending, you can purchase exactly what you need and book your trip now. This is especially valuable for those larger, dream trips that require significant point balances.

Never Miss Last-Minute Getaways

We've all been there – an incredible flight deal pops up, but your Aeroplan balance is too low to take advantage of it. When you have the ability to buy points instantly, you're always ready for spontaneous travel opportunities. Don't let a low balance stop you from jumping on time-sensitive deals or last-minute adventures.

Treat It Like a Travel Savings Fund

Think of buying Aeroplan points the same way you'd save up for any major purchase. You can strategically top up your account throughout the year, especially during promotions like this one, building your "travel fund" for future adventures. It's a proactive approach to ensuring you have the points you need when the perfect travel opportunity arises.

How Long Does It Take to Receive Points?

Point purchases through Aeroplan (processed by Points.com) typically post within 24-72 hours. In my personal experience purchasing points it has been nearly instantaneous.

However, If you're chasing a specific award availability, make sure to factor in this processing time before the seats disappear.
