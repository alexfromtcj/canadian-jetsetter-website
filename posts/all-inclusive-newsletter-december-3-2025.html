📝 News & Noteworthy: Air Transat 📝

Today, Air Transat pilots voted to strike if a new contract with the airline can’t be reached. This means as of December 10th, the roughly 700 pilots can strike, or management can impose a lockout. 

This doesn’t mean a strike is sure to happen. No strike notice has been issued and Air Transat’s operations are continuing as planned. But be aware of this if you’re thinking of booking an Air Transat vacation package or flights for this month.

For more up-to-date information, visit Air Transat’s website here.
