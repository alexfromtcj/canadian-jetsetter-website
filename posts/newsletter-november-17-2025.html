ONE MORE WEEK! Earn Up to 55,000 Avion Points with the RBC Avion Visa Infinite Card!

You have one more week before this amazing offer disappears for the RBC Avion Visa Infinite Card!

Until November 25, 2025, earn up to 55,000 Avion points (a value of up to approximately $1,100 CAD) when you apply and qualify for the offer.

See below for the juicy details of this card and its limited-time offer:

  • Earn 35,000 bonus Avion points upon approval + an extra 20,000 bonus points when you spend $5,000 in the first six months. 
  • Earn 1 Avion point for every $1 in purchases, and earn an additional 25% bonus points on eligible travel purchases. 
  • Use your points for travel on any airline, any flight, any time — no blackout periods.
  • Points never expire, so your Avion points are ready when you’re ready. 
  • Transfer your points to partner programs (such as airline loyalty programs) for flexibility. 
  • Comprehensive travel and other insurance coverage included: emergency medical, trip cancellation/interruption, delayed/ lost baggage, mobile device insurance, and more.
  • Visa Infinite card benefits, including hotel and dining perks and exclusive-event access.
