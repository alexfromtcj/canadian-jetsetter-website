Score Big: TD First Class Travel Visa Infinite Delivers Up to $1,400 in Value

Canadian travel enthusiasts, listen up! One of the year's most compelling credit card promotions has landed, and it's perfect timing for your 2026 travel planning. The TD First Class Travel® Visa Infinite Card is currently offering an elevated welcome package that transforms everyday spending into premium travel experiences.

What Makes This Offer Special?

This isn't your typical credit card signup bonus. TD is rolling out the red carpet with up to 165,000 TD Rewards Points, plus a first-year annual fee waiver – that's up to $1,400 in total value. Here's how the points break down:

  • 20,000 points just for making your first purchase
  • 145,000 points when you spend $7,500 within 180 days
  • Up to 10,000 birthday bonus points annually

But the perks don't stop at points. Cardholders also receive a $100 annual TD Travel Credit for Expedia bookings and 4 complimentary airport lounge visits through the Visa Airport Companion Program.

The All-Inclusive Vacation Strategy

Here's where this card truly shines for Canadian travellers: fixed-value points are perfect for all-inclusive vacations. While I typically reserve variable-value points for flights, TD Rewards' consistent redemption rate makes them ideal for resort bookings.

Through the Expedia for TD portal, I've spotted 4-star Mexican all-inclusive resorts starting at just 37,400 points per night.

Do the math – one card's welcome bonus could cover four or more nights at a premium resort. If your travel partner also applies, you're looking at 8+ nights of all-inclusive bliss!

Earning Power That Works for You

The TD First Class Travel Visa Infinite doesn't just reward travel spending. Its tiered earning structure means your everyday purchases contribute to your next getaway:

  • 8x points on Expedia for TD bookings
  • 6x points on groceries, dining, and public transit
  • 4x points on recurring bills and streaming services
  • 2x points on everything else

The Bottom Line

With no travel blackouts, no seat restrictions, and points that never expire (as long as your account stays active), this card offers genuine flexibility for Canadian travellers. The current promotion represents one of TD's strongest offers this year, making it an opportune time to maximize your travel rewards potential.

Ready to transform your spending into unforgettable travel experiences? This elevated welcome bonus won't last forever.
