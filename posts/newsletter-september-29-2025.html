NEW! Porter Airlines x American Airlines Partnership

Big news for Canadian travellers!

Porter Airlines now lets you book American Airlines flights directly through flyporter.com, unlocking seamless connections to Dallas Fort Worth, Charlotte, Philadelphia, and beyond. With easy baggage transfers and multiple connection hubs across North America, getting to your U.S. destination or even further abroad has never been simpler.

This is the kind of partnership that makes jet-setting a breeze!

BONUS POINTS! Virgin Atlantic Points Event

Attention, jetsetters! Virgin Atlantic is offering up to 70% bonus points when you buy Virgin Points now through October 12th, 2025. This is your chance to top up your balance and get closer to that dream redemption!

Here's how the bonus structure works:

  • 5K-24K points: 20% bonus
  • 25K-69K points: 40% bonus
  • 70K-124K points: 60% bonus
  • 125K-200K points: 70% bonus

There are some great deals going on right now with Virgin Points, especially from Toronto! It’s important to note that you can use them not only on Virgin Atlantic flights but also with their airline partners. 

For example, you can snag direct flights from Toronto to Amsterdam via KLM starting at just 12,000 Virgin Points plus taxes one way! 

Another example, you can book direct flights from London Heathrow to Toronto starting at just 10,000 Virgin Points plus taxes one way! 

Points cost £15/$25 per 1,000 points (plus a one-off transaction fee), and you can purchase up to 200,000 points per calendar year. 

Important note: You'll need at least one qualifying earning or spending transaction in your Flying Club account to be eligible. Brand new accounts without activity can't purchase points yet.

Ready to maximize your points? This promotion ends October 12th, so don't miss out!
