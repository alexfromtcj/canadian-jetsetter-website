Hey Travellers! ✈️💸

This week’s newsletter kicks off with an important update for soccer fans: FIFA World Cup 26™ ticket prices are now out, and the lottery to purchase tickets is officially open. If attending the World Cup is on your bucket list, this is your window to apply for FIFA’s random selection draw before the January 13, 2026 deadline.

And of course… the deals this week are HOT 🔥
We’re talking Canada from $95, the U.S. from $251, Europe from $589, and Asia from $756.

As always, dates are limited and availability can change quickly — so if something catches your eye, it’s worth checking sooner rather than later. Let’s dive into this week’s best fares. ✈️

⚽ FIFA World Cup 26™ Ticket Prices + Lottery to Buy Tickets Out Now!

A reminder for all you FIFA World Cup fans out there!

You have until January 13, 2026 to apply to FIFA’s random selection draw. If you’re selected, you’ll have the opportunity to purchase tickets.

Prices are quite expensive depending on the game but this historic tournament will feature 48 teams playing 104 matches across three host countries, making it the greatest World Cup yet.
