WestJet Just Made Summer 2026 WAY More Exciting for Canadians ✈️

If you’re already daydreaming about your 2026 vacations, WestJet just gave us a whole lot more reasons to get excited. The airline just announced its summer 2026 schedule, and spoiler alert: it’s full of new destinations, non-stop connections, and some seriously cool routes for both East and West Coast travellers.

Toronto Takes Off to South America (and Beyond)

Toronto travellers, this one’s for you. WestJet is giving Toronto Pearson International Airport its first-ever non-stop flight to South America: Medellín, Colombia. Imagine exploring Colombia’s colorful streets and coffee culture without the multi-stop hassle.

But that’s not all — Toronto is also getting seasonal flights to Cardiff, UK, the Azores (Ponta Delgada, Portugal), and a return to Glasgow, Scotland. That’s four new ways to explore Europe (and Colombia) directly from Canada’s largest city, all on WestJet’s extended-range Boeing 737 MAX aircraft.

  • Toronto → Medellín: 4x weekly, starting April 28, 2026
  • Toronto → Cardiff: 4x weekly, starting May 22, 2026
  • Toronto → Ponta Delgada: 4x weekly, starting June 12, 2026
  • Toronto → Glasgow: 4x weekly, starting May 15, 2026

Halifax: Canada’s Atlantic Gateway

East Coasters, you’re in for a treat. Halifax is leveling up as Canada’s Atlantic hub with four new seasonal routes: Madrid, Lisbon, Copenhagen, and even Detroit for those quick transborder trips. Shorter flights, competitive pricing, and easy access to Europe — Halifax just became the ultimate springboard for summer adventures.

  • Halifax → Madrid: 4x weekly, May 15, 2026
  • Halifax → Lisbon: 4x weekly, May 1, 2026
  • Halifax → Copenhagen: 4x weekly, May 28, 2026
  • Halifax → Detroit: 7x weekly, May 18, 2026

Western Canada Gets Some Love Too

WestJet isn’t forgetting the prairies! Edmonton and Winnipeg now have non-stop access to Reykjavík, Iceland, opening up epic adventure opportunities for Canadians chasing the Northern Lights or Icelandic landscapes.

  • Edmonton → Reykjavík: 1x weekly, June 26, 2026
  • Winnipeg → Reykjavík: 1x weekly, June 27, 2026

Plus, there are new domestic routes for summer 2026: Winnipeg → London, ON (3x weekly) and Vancouver → Penticton, BC (5x weekly). Perfect for weekend getaways or connecting through Canada.

Sun, Fun, Repeat

WestJet Vacations and Sunwing Vacations are expanding year-round flights from Toronto, Montreal, Quebec City, Calgary, and Winnipeg. So if you’re thinking about a winter sun getaway, there are more options to pick from!

  • Montreal → Puerto Plata / Samana: 1x weekly
  • Toronto → Puerto Plata: 1x weekly
  • Quebec City → Cayo Coco: 1x weekly
  • Winnipeg → Cancun: 1x weekly
  • Calgary → Mazatlán / Guadalajara: 1x weekly

Whether you’re craving South American flair, European culture, or Icelandic adventure, WestJet’s 2026 summer lineup has something for every Canadian traveller. Time to start planning those trips — passports at the ready!
