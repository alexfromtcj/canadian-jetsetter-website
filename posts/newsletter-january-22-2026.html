Increased Welcome Bonus! RBC Avion Visa Infinite Card

One of the most versatile travel credit cards just increased its welcome bonus!

The RBC Avion Visa Infinite Card now offers up to 55,000 Avion points with approval and qualifying spend, which is near the top of what this program offers.

Avion shines because points can be transferred to airline partners like British Airways, Cathay Pacific, American Airlines, and WestJet, often with limited-time transfer bonuses.

If flexible airline points matter to you, this is a strong offer to keep in mind.
