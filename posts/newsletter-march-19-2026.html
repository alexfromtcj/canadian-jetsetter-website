✈️🇨🇦 Flight Deals from $197 + Exciting AMEX x Flying Blue Transfer Bonus!

Hey Travellers! 

Some exciting news in the aviation industry that affects anyone who lives or plans on visiting Montréal, Québec in the future!

You’ve likely heard of Montréal–Trudeau International Airport, but what about Montréal Metropolitan Airport? Located just 15 km from downtown, it currently serves regional routes within Québec and the Maritimes. In 2023, Porter Airlines announced plans to build a new passenger terminal here, opening the door to flights across Canada from a second Montréal airport. Next week, the airport is hosting an event to showcase the new terminal, giving a first look at Porter’s expansion plans. This is an exciting development for Montréal and Canadian travellers in general, so I’ll keep you posted as more details drop.

On another exciting note, there is an amazing American Express x Flying Blue Air France KLM transfer bonus live right now that you need to know about. Read below to learn more!

In terms of airfare, WestJet and Air Canada both have sales on currently. If you’re looking at booking a flight in the next couple days, these could help you shave off a few bucks. Read further below to learn more.

And now… the flight deals. 

Think round trip fares from $197 within Canada, $379 to Sun destinations, $532 to Europe, and Aeroplan redemptions starting at 15,000 points + fees one way. 

As always, prices and availability can change quickly. If something lines up with your 2026 travel plans, it is worth taking a closer look sooner rather than later.

Let’s dive into this week’s best fares. ✈️

Amazing Points News! American Express x Flying Blue Bonus Points Event!

Exciting news for American Express credit card holders and those who collect Flying Blue Miles! Until April 17th, you can transfer Amex Membership Rewards to Flying Blue Miles at a 1 → 1.25 rate.

For context, Amex Membership Rewards used to transfer to Flying Blue at a 1 → 0.75 rate. They recently bumped that up to a 1:1 ratio, and right now there’s this additional 25% transfer bonus on top of that. That means you’re effectively getting 1.25 Flying Blue miles for every Amex point you transfer. That’s a massive swing in value!
