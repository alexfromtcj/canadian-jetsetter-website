Limited-Time Offer: Earn Up to 55,000 Avion Points with the RBC Avion Visa Infinite Card!

If you’ve been dreaming of turning everyday spending into your next big getaway, the RBC Avion Visa Infinite card might just be your new travel companion. It’s one of Canada’s most flexible and rewarding travel credit cards — and right now, there’s a limited-time offer ending November 25, 2025 that’s perfect for frequent flyers and future jetsetters alike.

The Limited-Time Welcome Offer

This offer is one of the most generous we’ve seen from RBC in recent years. When you’re approved for the card, you’ll earn 35,000 Avion Points instantly. Then, when you spend $5,000 within your first six months, you’ll unlock an additional 20,000 Avion Points.

That’s a total of 55,000 Avion Points, which can easily translate up to $1,100 CAD in travel value† when redeemed strategically. Considering the annual fee is only $120, this welcome bonus delivers an incredible return — especially for a premium card.

Why Travellers Love Avion Points

Avion Rewards are known for one thing above all else: flexibility. You can redeem your points for any flight, on any airline, at any time. There are no blackout dates, and you’re not locked into one specific carrier. Whether you’re flying to Toronto for a weekend getaway or booking a bucket-list trip to Europe, your points are always ready to go.

And if you prefer to transfer points to airline partners like British Airways Avios or WestJet Rewards, you can unlock even higher value — perfect for those who love finding “sweet spot” redemptions.

Everyday Earning Power

The RBC Avion Visa Infinite also makes it easy to keep earning once the bonus is unlocked. You’ll collect 1.25 Avion Points per dollar on travel purchases (airlines, accommodations, tour operators, cruise lines, travel agencies and car rental companies) and 1 point per dollar on everything else.

That means your groceries, gas, and dinners out all move you closer to your next adventure. The earn rate is consistent, simple, and rewarding — no need to juggle complicated category bonuses or rotating offers.

Premium Perks and Protection

Beyond the points, the card comes packed with travel insurance and premium Visa Infinite benefits. You’ll enjoy:

  • Out-of-province emergency medical insurance
  • Trip cancellation and interruption insurance
  • Flight delay and lost baggage protection
  • Mobile device insurance
  • Access to the Visa Infinite Concierge, dining perks, and exclusive travel experiences

In other words, it’s not just a rewards card — it’s a reliable travel partner that’s there to back you up when plans change or luggage goes missing.

Who This Card Is Best For

The RBC Avion Visa Infinite is ideal for travellers who:

  • Want the freedom to book travel their way — no blackout dates or restrictions
  • Prefer flexible redemptions and airline transfer options
  • Appreciate strong travel insurance without paying ultra-premium fees
  • Plan to meet the $5,000 spend requirement within six months to unlock the full bonus

If that sounds like you, this card will fit seamlessly into your travel-rewards strategy.

Final Thoughts

Between its massive welcome bonus, flexible redemption options, and comprehensive travel coverage, the RBC Avion Visa Infinite stands out as one of Canada’s most well-rounded travel cards. It’s perfect for travellers who want to earn big, book freely, and enjoy peace of mind along the way.

If you’ve been waiting for the right moment to jump into the world of Avion Rewards, this is it. Don’t wait too long — the 55,000-point offer ends November 25, 2025.

Apply now and get ready to make your next trip a little closer than you think.
