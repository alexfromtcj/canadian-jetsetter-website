✈️ Air Transat Expands Its Horizons: Six Exciting New Destinations for 2025–26

Air Transat is spreading its wings again — and this time, they’re reaching six brand-new destinations across four continents. From vibrant Latin American cities to the shores of the Atlantic, Canadians will soon have even more ways to explore the world directly from home.

Here’s what’s new in Air Transat’s global lineup:

☕ Medellín, Colombia

Service begins Nov 1, 2025
From: Toronto (via Cartagena 2× weekly)

Known as the “City of Eternal Spring,” Medellín is quickly becoming one of South America’s most popular destinations. Expect colourful neighbourhoods, modern cable cars that glide over the hills, and a creative scene that rivals any in Latin America.

🕌 Istanbul, Türkiye

Service begins Dec 6, 2025
From: Toronto (2× weekly direct)

Where East meets West, Istanbul dazzles with centuries of history, vibrant bazaars, and rivers of Turkish coffee. Air Transat’s new nonstop route makes it easier than ever to step into the crossroads of civilizations.

🌮 Guadalajara, Mexico

Service begins Dec 13, 2025
From: Montréal (2× weekly direct)

Beyond the beaches, Guadalajara is the cultural heart of Mexico — the birthplace of mariachi and tequila. With its colourful markets, elegant plazas, and thriving food scene, it’s a must-visit for travellers looking to explore Mexico beyond the resort towns.

🌿 Georgetown, Guyana

Service begins Dec 16, 2025
From: Toronto (2× weekly direct)

A destination that’s long flown under the radar, Georgetown offers a unique Caribbean-meets-South-America charm. With lush rainforests, colonial architecture, and waterfalls that rival any in the world, Guyana is poised to become a new favourite for adventurous travellers.

🌴 Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Service begins Feb 4, 2026
From: Toronto (2× weekly) | Montréal (1× weekly)

One of the world’s most energetic cities is coming within easy reach. With direct service to Rio, Canadians can soak in the sun at Copacabana, hike up to Christ the Redeemer, and experience the buzz of Brazilian culture — all without a single connection.

☀️ Agadir, Morocco

Service begins June 12, 2026
From: Montréal (1× weekly)

Morocco's premier beach destination offers year-round sunshine, golden Atlantic coastline, and a relaxed resort vibe. Explore the bustling souk, savour fresh seafood and tagines, and use Agadir as your gateway to the Atlas Mountains, historic Essaouira, and the vibrant souks of Marrakech.

🌎 Jetsetter Take

With these additions, Air Transat continues to prove it’s more than just a vacation airline — it’s becoming a key connector between Canada and the world’s most inspiring cities. Whether you’re craving samba in Brazil, history in Türkiye, or golden beaches in Morocco, your next adventure just got a lot closer to home.
