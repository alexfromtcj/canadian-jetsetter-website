Flying Blue December Promo: Ottawa’s Best Deal to Europe

Flying Blue Promo Rewards are one of the easiest ways Canadians can save big when booking Air France or KLM flights with Miles. Each month, Flying Blue releases new discounted routes — and for December, one Canadian city gets the spotlight:

🇨🇦 Ottawa → Europe from just 18,750 Miles (Economy)

A full 25% off the usual reward pricing.

If you're based in Ottawa or willing to fly from there, this is one of the cheapest Europe redemptions you’ll find all winter and spring.

Here’s what Canadian travellers need to know.

✈️ Use Your Miles for Any Available Seat

Flying Blue lets you redeem Miles for any available seat on Air France, KLM, and partner airlines. No separate award inventory to fight for — if a seat is open, you can book it.

Reward tickets:

  • Can be booked for anyone
  • Work on Air France, KLM, and partners
  • Require taxes and surcharges (paid separately)
  • Don’t earn Miles or XP
  • Follow Flying Blue and airline fare rules

🌟 What Makes Promo Rewards Special

Promo Rewards offer up to 25% off standard Miles pricing, making your points stretch further.
New offers are released on the 1st of every month, with December bookings valid for travel until 31 May 2026.

As mentioned, this month’s Canadian highlight is:

Ottawa → Europe

From 18,750 Miles (Economy). Includes direct flights to Paris, as well as connecting flights to other areas of Europe via Paris.

Great for:

  • Spring Europe trips
  • Affordable winter getaways
  • Positioning to Paris before moving onward into the EU

✨ Flying Blue Extra: More Deals for Subscribers

Flying Blue Extra subscribers get access to exclusive Promo Rewards marked “Extra Exclusive.”

Benefits include:

  • More discounted routes
  • More availability
  • Earlier access to limited promotions

If you fly Air France/KLM often, the additional discounted redemptions can easily offset the subscription cost.

🧾 Key Conditions to Know

Before booking, remember:

  • Taxes & carrier surcharges are not covered by Miles
  • Promo Rewards are subject to limited availability
  • La Première awards require Platinum or Ultimate status and are extremely limited
  • Infant bookings require contacting Flying Blue Customer Service
  • Promo Rewards follow fare rules and Flying Blue program terms

⭐ Why This Matters for Canadian Jetsetters

Even though only Ottawa is featured in this month’s Promo Rewards, the value is still strong:

  • 18,750 Miles is one of the lowest ways to reach Europe from Canada
  • Easy connections throughout Europe
  • Perfect for travellers with Amex Membership Rewards, which are transferable to Flying Blue at a 1:0.75 ratio
  • Great option for anyone willing to fly from Ottawa for a cheap redemption

If Europe is on your 2026 radar, this is a great month to book.
