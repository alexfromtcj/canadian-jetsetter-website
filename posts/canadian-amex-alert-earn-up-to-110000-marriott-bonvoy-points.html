Canadian AMEX Alert: Earn Up to 110,000 Marriott Bonvoy Points!

If you’ve been thinking about upping your hotel points game, now is the time. American Express Canada has just more than doubled the welcome bonus on both the Marriott Bonvoy® American Express®  and Marriott Bonvoy® Business American Express® cards.

You can now earn up to 110,000 Marriott Bonvoy points, one of the strongest offers we’ve seen in Canada. But what does that really mean in terms of value? Let’s break it down.

This offer ends April 7, 2026.

Marriott Bonvoy® American Express®

  • Total Welcome Bonus: Up to 110,000 points
    • 80,000 points after spending $6,000 in the first 6 months
    • 30,000 points after making a purchase in the 15th month
  • Annual Fee: $120
  • Earning Rates:
    • 5x points at participating Marriott Bonvoy hotels
    • 2x points on all other purchases
  • Perks:
    • Annual Free Night Award up to 35,000 points
    • Automatic Silver Elite status and 15 Elite Night Credits yearly
    • Opportunity to reach Gold Elite after $30,000 in purchases or qualifying stays

Example Value: The W Toronto currently charges around 35,000 points per night. That means your welcome bonus alone could cover three free nights, worth over $1,000, for a card that costs just $120 per year. Then, every year after, your annual free night could be worth $400 or more at top Marriott hotels.

Marriott Bonvoy® Business American Express®

  • Total Welcome Bonus: Up to 110,000 points
    • 80,000 points after spending $10,000 in the first 6 months
    • 30,000 points after making a purchase in the 15th month
  • Annual Fee: $150
  • Earning Rates:
    • 5x points at participating Marriott Bonvoy hotels
    • 3x points on gas, dining, and travel
    • 2x points on all other purchases
  • Perks:
    • Annual Free Night Award up to 35,000 points
    • Automatic Silver Elite status and 15 Elite Night Credits yearly
    • Gold Elite status after $30,000 in purchases or 10 qualifying paid nights plus Elite Night Credits
    • Employee card controls and full business management tools

The Business card is ideal if you run a business or spend heavily on travel, dining, or gas, as it maximizes points earning potential across multiple categories.

Why This Offer Stands Out

  1. One of Canada’s only co-branded hotel credit cards, few alternatives can deliver this kind of hotel points value.
  2. Flexible points redemption, no blackout dates at over 7,000 hotels worldwide.
  3. Annual Free Night Award, keeps delivering value year after year.
  4. Elite status perks, complimentary Silver Elite, with a clear path to Gold.

Even if you are new to hotel points, these offers are extremely user-friendly and easy to maximize.

Quick Tips to Maximize Value

  • Plan your $6,000 (Personal) or $10,000 (Business) spending strategically in the first 6 months to unlock the 80,000-point portion of the bonus.
  • Use your annual free night at high-value properties such as W Toronto, JW Marriott, or other hotels charging 30,000–35,000 points per night.
  • Keep the card long-term to take advantage of yearly free nights and continued elite status benefits.

Final Thoughts

Personally, I have held the Personal Marriott Bonvoy Amex for a few years now, and it has consistently delivered excellent value. With the welcome bonus more than doubling to 110,000 points, there has never been a better time for Canadians to get in on these cards.
