Hey Travellers!

Are you a “really bad” photographer who is interested in Iceland?

Icelandair has a fun contest running where they’re searching for a “really bad photographer” to prove that no matter how limited your photography skills are, Iceland will still make your photos look great. You could win a 10-day planned trip with travel expenses covered, alongside $50,000 USD (~$69,000 CAD) for your photographs, content, and participation.

Only amateur photographers are allowed to enter their name. Ensure to read all the Terms & Conditions before entering. Contest is open until April 30th, 2026.

Click here to enter, and good luck!
