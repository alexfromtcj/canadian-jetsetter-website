5 Travel Credit Cards to Consider for Summer Travel in 2026

Summer travel is approaching quickly, and if you are planning a trip in the coming months, now can be a great time to think about applying for a travel credit card.

Many Canadian travel credit cards offer generous welcome bonuses, which can help you earn points quickly. By applying now, you may have enough time to earn the welcome bonus and redeem points for flights, hotels, or travel experiences before summer travel begins.

The best travel credit card for you depends on your goals. Some cards offer flexible travel rewards, while others provide airline perks, airport lounge access, or strong earning rates on everyday spending.

Here are five travel credit cards available in Canada that stand out in 2026, each offering unique benefits depending on your travel style.

Critical Warnings Every Canadian Should Know

Before jumping into any of these cards, a few rules matter:

  • Never carry a balance just to earn points
  • Set calendar reminders before the second-year fee posts
  • Only apply for cards where you can naturally hit the minimum spend
  • Use your points; don’t hoard them while programs quietly devalue

These cards are tools, not traps — but only if used correctly.

The Bottom Line

The right travel credit card can make a meaningful difference when planning your next trip.

If you are planning a summer trip, applying for a travel credit card now can give you time to meet the minimum spending requirements and earn the welcome bonus before your departure. In many cases, these points can be redeemed for flights, hotel stays, or travel purchases, helping stretch your travel budget further.

Before applying, it is always worth considering your travel habits, income requirements, and how you plan to redeem points.

Choosing the card that best aligns with your travel style can help you get the most value from your rewards.

Happy travels!
