FlyingBlue November 2025 Promo: Business Class to Europe from 45,000 Miles

Canadians, here’s a travel hack you won’t want to miss: Air France and KLM have just dropped some of their business class award flights by 25% this November, including routes from Toronto and Ottawa.

This means you can fly business class to almost any European destination for as low as 45,000 FlyingBlue miles—and yes, that includes connections through Paris or Amsterdam. These deals are part of the FlyingBlue Promo Rewards, released monthly with discounted award flights.

How to Book

To take advantage of these great deals, head over to www.airfrance.ca/en and search for flights under the “Book with Miles” option. This will allow you to see all available FlyingBlue reward flights and take advantage of the November promo discounts.

Keep in mind, promo rewards must be booked in November, but you have the flexibility to choose your travel dates anytime up to April 30, 2026, giving you plenty of options to plan your European adventure around your schedule.

As a FlyingBlue member, you can use your miles to book any seat on the plane, from economy to business class, including reward tickets that are exclusively available to members. This means you can customize your trip exactly how you want it—whether that’s flying in luxurious business class, mixing cabin types for multi-city journeys, or simply stretching your miles for maximum value.

November Promo Deals for Canadians

Excitingly, there are multiple Canadian promos offered this month, giving travellers an incredible opportunity to explore Europe at some of the lowest mileage rates of the year.

  • Air France Business Class
    • Europe ↔ Ottawa: 45,000 miles (25% off)
    • Europe ↔ Toronto: 45,000 miles (25% off)
  • KLM Business Class
    • Europe ↔ Toronto: 45,000 miles (25% off)
  • Air France and KLM Economy Class
    • Europe ↔ Montreal: 18,750 miles (25% off)

These deals represent some of the lowest business and economy class mileage prices you’ll see, so whether you’re dreaming of sipping wine in Paris, wandering the canals of Amsterdam, or exploring historic European cities, this is your chance to book your adventure in style—and at an unbeatable value.

How to Earn FlyingBlue Miles in Canada

The easiest way for Canadians to access these deals is through American Express Membership Rewards points. You can convert AMEX points to FlyingBlue miles at a 1:0.75 ratio:

  • 1,000 AMEX points → 750 FlyingBlue miles

This is one of several transfer partners for AMEX points, making it simple to stock up your account and grab these promo awards.

Extra Perks

Promo reward tickets include connecting flights within Europe, either outbound or return, through Paris or Amsterdam.

By keeping your travel dates and cities flexible, you can unlock even better availability—so be sure to book early before these deals disappear!

Quick Tips to Maximize Your Miles

  1. Mix business and economy classes for multi-city trips.
  2. Check multiple travel dates to find the lowest mileage fares.
  3. If you’re short on miles, transfer AMEX points to top up your FlyingBlue balance quickly.

45,000 miles for business class to Europe is about as low as it gets. Book in November, travel through April 2026, and turn your miles into a dream European getaway.
