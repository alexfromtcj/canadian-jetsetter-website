Points are up and fares are down.

If hotel points are part of your 2026 strategy, this is worth a serious look before the April 7, 2026 deadline.

And now… the flight deals.

Think quick hops in Canada from $141 round trip, southern destinations from $374, Europe from $599, and Asia from $767.

Reminder! Increased Points Offer for Marriott Bonvoy American Express Cards

In case you somehow missed this awesome news… well, here you are!

This is great news if you’re looking to earn points for one of Canada’s only co-branded hotel credit cards.

The Marriott Bonvoy American Express and Marriott Bonvoy Business American Express cards both have new limited time offers, giving you the opportunity to earn up to 110,000 Marriott Bonvoy Points. This offer ends April 7, 2026.

Both cards earn 5x points at participating Marriott hotels and include an annual Free Night Award (up to 35,000 points), with the Business version offering higher everyday earn rates and the Personal card featuring a lower annual fee and spending requirement.

This offer is a strong one to consider, but you need to make sure you’re redeeming strategically!

To learn more about the strategy involved with these cards and how to make the most of them, check out my video below for more insight.

Note: At the time of this video’s filming, the offer was only up to 50,000 Marriott Bonvoy points. The current offer showcased is more than double this.
